## Top 10 Gaps — Ranked by Opportunity

### 1. AI Agents (Gap: 10/10) 🔥

**Competitors:** 1 (Griffain)

Griffain is the only AI agent tool in the entire Solana ecosystem. It's limited:
no templates, no backtesting, no multi-agent support, no no-code builder.

**What to build:**

- AI agent platform with 6 pre-built strategy templates
- No-code agent builder (drag-and-drop strategy creation)
- Backtesting on historical data before deploying capital
- Social leaderboard: see top-performing agents, copy them
- SaaS + performance fees = compounding revenue model

**Revenue model:** $30–100/mo SaaS + 15% of agent profits

---

### 2. Airdrop Tools (Gap: 9/10) 🔥

**Competitors:** 2 (Drops, Airdropped.link — both basic listing sites)

Every Solana user wants free tokens. Neither existing tool has an eligibility
checker, farming guide, or alert system.

**What to build:**

- Wallet eligibility checker (paste address → see what you qualify for)
- Airdrop calendar with confirmed + speculative drops
- Step-by-step farming guides to qualify for upcoming airdrops
- Telegram/email alerts when new airdrops open claiming
- Portfolio impact estimator (estimated value of pending drops)

**Revenue model:** Freemium + $10–30/mo premium

**March 2026 timing:** Multiple major protocols expected to drop Q1–Q2 2026.
Build this now.

---

### 3. Wallet Trackers (Gap: 8/10) 🔥

**Competitors:** 4 (SpyBot, RayBot, KolScan + 1 — all basic)

Every active trader monitors wallets daily. Only 4 tools exist and all are
primitive. Orangie charges $40–200/mo for his Potion Tracker with thousands
of paying subscribers.

**What to build:**

- Track up to 200 wallets with real-time Telegram alerts
- Smart money discovery via Nansen integration
- PnL analysis per wallet: win rate, avg hold time, ROI
- Copy-trade execution tier (auto-mirror a wallet's trades)
- AI insights: "This wallet has 85% win rate on memecoins"

**Revenue model:** Tiered $20/$75/$200 per month

---

### 4. Token Safety / Rug Checkers (Gap: 8/10) 🔥

**Competitors:** 14 tools across two categories — ALL single-purpose

RugCheck, Solsniffer, QuillCheck, Soul Scanner, Bubblemaps — each does one
thing. No combined API. No Telegram-native tool (they're all websites). No
bundle detection specifically.

**What to build:**

- Single API: contract scanner + rug check + volume authenticity + bundle detect
- Checks: mint authority, freeze authority, LP lock, dev wallet %, bundle
  signatures (Vortex/Kinesis/PumpKing), wash trading, holder distribution,
  First-20 analysis
- Telegram bot: `/scan <address>` → instant report in 2 seconds
- Embeddable widget for trading terminals (B2B revenue)

**Revenue model:** API licensing ($50–500/mo) + Telegram sub ($10–30/mo) + widget licensing ($100–500/mo per terminal integrated).

---

### 5. Casinos & Gambling (Gap: 7/10)

**Competitors:** 13 — but none offer white-label. All are standalone products.

SolPump.io and Degen Coin Flip prove on-chain casino works. Open-source
Anchor contracts exist. MagicBlock Ephemeral Rollups give sub-second rounds
with zero gas — an edge no existing casino has.

**What to build:**

- On-chain casino: Crash + Coinflip with ORAO VRF (provably fair)
- MagicBlock integration for sub-second game rounds, gasless for players
- Token integration: memecoin projects use their token as betting currency
- White-label engine: influencers deploy branded casino, you take 20% of house
- Affiliate program: projects/influencers get custom casino pages

**Revenue model:** 3% house edge (passive) + 20% of white-label house revenue

---

### 6. Launchpads (Gap: 7/10)

**Competitors:** 9 — pump.fun dominates but Believe, Moonit, BONKfun growing

**March 2026 update:** PumpSwap launched (DEX integration) + creator revenue
sharing is live. The space is still fracturing. A launchpad with anti-rug
defaults baked in from day 1 has a clearer moat now.

**What to build:**

- Bonding curve launchpad with mandatory rug score on every token
- Creator reputation system: track record of previous launches visible
- Revenue sharing from day 1 (pump.fun just added this)
- Bundler detection built into the UI

**Revenue model:** 1% bonding curve fee on all trades

### 7. Crypto Tax (Gap: 7/10)

**Competitors:** 6 — ALL are multi-chain generalists that get Solana wrong

Awaken, Koinly, Netrunner: missing pump.fun transactions, wrong cost basis
on bonding curve tokens, broken compressed NFT handling.

**What to build:**

- Purpose-built Solana tax calculator
- pump.fun aware: correct bonding curve buy/sell cost basis
- Airdrop handling: fair market value at receipt time
- LP/staking: correctly tracks impermanent loss, staking rewards
- Compressed NFT support
- Export: TurboTax, H&R Block, CPA-ready reports

**Revenue model:** Annual subscription $49–$149/yr. Tax season = 3–5x spike.

### 8. Derivs/Perps (Gap: 7/10)

No aggregator exists. Each platform is siloed.

**Revenue model:** 0.05–0.1% per trade routed, or subscription

**Revenue model:** Freemium + $10–30/mo premium analytics
